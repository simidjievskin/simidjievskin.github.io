I am an Associate Professor of AI for Healthcare with the [DIG (Data, Intelligence and Graphs) team](https://dig.telecom-paris.fr){:target="\_blank"} at [Télécom Paris](https://www.telecom-paris.fr/){:target="\_blank"}, [Institut Polytechnique de Paris](https://www.ip-paris.fr/){:target="\_blank"}, France. I am also a Visiting Fellow at the University of Cambridge, UK, where I collaborate with [Prof. Jean Abraham](https://crukcambridgecentre.org.uk/research/programmes/breast-cancer/pbci){:target="\_blank"} on multimodal data analysis for breast cancer diagnosis, prognosis, and treatment planning at the Department of Oncology. In addition, I collaborate with [Prof. Mateja Jamnik](https://www.cl.cam.ac.uk/~mj201){:target="\_blank"} and [Prof. Pietro Liò](https://www.cl.cam.ac.uk/~pl219/){:target="\_blank"} at the [Department of Computer Science and Technology](https://www.cst.cam.ac.uk/){:target="\_blank"}. I am partly affiliated with the [Department of Knowledge Technologies, Jožef Stefan Institute](http://kt.ijs.si){:target="\_blank"}, Slovenia. My research interests lie in machine learning and data mining, with a focus on multimodal approaches and their applications in medicine, biology, and neuroscience.

{: style="text-align: justify"}
I am a co-founder of [Bias Variance Labs](https://www.bvlabs.ai){:target="\_blank"}, a research-driven company that specializes in state-of-the-art AI solutions (data stewardship, data & knowledge representation, data analyses, machine learning & reasoning) for on space research & technology.
