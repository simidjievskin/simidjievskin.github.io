{: style="text-align: justify"}
I am an Associate Professor of AI for Healthcare in the [DIG (Data, Intelligence and Graphs) team](https://dig.telecom-paris.fr) at [Télécom Paris](https://www.telecom-paris.fr/), [Institut Polytechnique de Paris](https://www.ip-paris.fr/), France. I am also a Visiting Fellow at the University of Cambridge, UK, where I collaborate with [Prof. Jean Abraham](https://www.oncology.cam.ac.uk/research/our-research/abraham) on multimodal data analysis for breast cancer diagnosis, prognosis, and treatment planning. In addition, I collaborate with [Prof. Mateja Jamnik](https://www.cl.cam.ac.uk/~mj201) and [Prof. Pietro Liò](https://www.cl.cam.ac.uk/~pl219/) at the [Department of Computer Science and Technology, University of Cambridge](https://www.cst.cam.ac.uk/). I am partly affiliated with the [Department of Knowledge Technologies, Jožef Stefan Institute](http://kt.ijs.si), Slovenia. My research interests lie in machine learning and data mining, with a focus on multimodal approaches and their applications in medicine, biology, and neuroscience.

{: style="text-align: justify"}
I am a co-founder of [Bias Variance Labs](https://www.bvlabs.ai), a research-driven company that specializes in state-of-the-art AI solutions (data stewardship, data & knowledge representation, data analyses, machine learning & reasoning) for on space research & technology.
